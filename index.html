Design For Life

Lifeward Labs is the business face of Michael Peiman's web development work.

Our mission is to create tools and resources which help empower human creativity and optimize human potential.

Our unique focus is on building and integrating tools and systems of personal productivity and knowledge management in the big-picture context of purpose-driven living and a meaningful life.

The immediate work of Lifeward Labs is to build MVP (Minimum Viable Product) "modules" of the various functions of a life-management system. These will be stand-alone apps, but designed with future integration or consolidation in mind.
